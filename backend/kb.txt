ABC Insurance – Policy Knowledge Base

Plan Types:

Silver Plan
- Coverage Limit: ₹500,000
- Includes: Basic hospitalization, general surgeries, doctor consultations
- Excludes: Cosmetic procedures, non-essential treatments
- Co-pay: 10%

Gold Plan
- Coverage Limit: ₹1,600,000
- Includes: All Silver benefits + specialist consultations + advanced surgeries
- Excludes: Experimental treatments, out-of-network facilities
- Co-pay: 5%

Platinum Plan
- Coverage Limit: ₹2,200,000
- Includes: All Gold benefits + ICU, premium room, chronic care management
- Excludes: Non-medically necessary treatments
- Co-pay: 0%

General Rules:
- Pre-authorization required for planned surgeries.
- Claims exceeding plan limits must be rejected.
- All treatments must be medically necessary and properly documented.
